United Nations Human Rights Council

The Russo-Ukraine War

COMMITTEE OVERVIEW

The UN Human Rights Council (UNHRC) convenes to address the Russo-Ukraine War, a critical global concern. Delegates will analyze human rights implications, including civilian casualties, displacement, and potential war crimes. The committee seeks resolutions fostering diplomatic dialogue, upholding human rights, and restoring regional stability. Discussions will focus on humanitarian concerns, ensuring accountability for all parties. The UNHRC provides a platform for collaborative diplomacy, urging member states to prioritize human rights in crafting solutions for this pressing international crisis.

Further inquiries regarding this committee, position papers, and general questions can be submitted to jhssmun.unhrc@gmail.com.
