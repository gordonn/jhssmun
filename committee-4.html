United Nations Human Rights Council

Child Labor in the DRC

COMMITTEE OVERVIEW

Explore the United Nations Human Rights Committee's focus on "Child Labor in the Democratic Republic of the Congo." Delegates will address challenges associated with child labor, particularly in sectors like mining and agriculture. The committee aims to understand the root causes, socio-economic implications, and human rights violations in the region. Delegates will work collaboratively to propose solutions and gain insights into global efforts to eradicate child labor, emphasizing the link between human rights and sustainable development. Suitable for both novice and experienced delegates, this committee offers a dynamic Model UN experience.

Further inquiries regarding this committee, position papers, and general questions can be submitted to jhssmun.unhrc@gmail.com.
