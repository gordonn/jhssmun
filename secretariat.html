Rebecca Zhang

Director General

Currently a senior IB student at Johnston Heights Secondary, Rebecca is delighted to serve as Director General for the second iteration of JHSSMUN. Since first entering the world of Model UN in 9th grade, her interest and concern for diplomacy and global affairs has only grown. For Rebecca, MUN has cultivated unforgettable memories and irreplaceable friendships, which she will always cherish; she hopes that this year’s iteration of JHSSMUN will be the same for you. Outside of Model UN, Rebecca can be found grinding her IB coursework, or struggling to manage competitive swim and piano. She waits eagerly for the conference and looks forward to meeting everyone!

Zahran Mohammad

USG of Media Affairs

A junior at Johnston Heights Secondary School, Zahran is excited to serve as the USG of Media Affairs for the JHSSMUN 2024. Since his first Model United Nations conference in Grade 10, he has been fascinated by the intricacies of this endeavor. Transitioning from a novice delegate to a member of the Secretariat team, Zahran found his voice in diplomacy, turning curiosity into commitment. He aims to use his passion for media to promote Model UN, seeing it as a transformative initiative for high schoolers to enhance public speaking and critical thinking skills while gaining a better understanding of global affairs.
