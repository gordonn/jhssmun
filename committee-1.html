Historical Crisis Committee

The Six Day War

COMMITTEE OVERVIEW

Step into the Historical Crisis Committee (HCC), where delegates are transported to a pivotal moment in history—the Six-Day War. This historical conflict, with significant regional implications, unfolds as delegates assume the roles of key players in the international arena.

Set against the backdrop of geopolitical tensions, humanitarian concerns, and potential security threats, delegates in this HCC session must grapple with the intricate complexities of the Six-Day War. Their decisions and actions will shape the course of events during this critical period. The committee challenges participants to navigate historical dilemmas, employ strategic thinking, and collaboratively devise solutions to mitigate the impact of the conflict and bring stability to the region. This HCC committee promises a dynamic and immersive Model UN experience for delegates seeking to engage with the complexities of a historical crisis.

Further inquiries regarding this committee, position papers, and general questions can be submitted to jhssmunhcc@gmail.com.
