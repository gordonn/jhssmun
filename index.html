Dear Prospective Delegates and Faculty Advisors

My name is Dilara Priyankarage, and I am so incredibly honoured to welcome you to the second iteration of JHSSMUN as Secretary General. My team and I have been working diligently to bring you a conference that will allow you to enjoy entertaining debate and build strong connections, making memories all the while. With that, we are delighted to invite you to join us at Johnston Heights Secondary School for this experience on February 24th, 2024!

Having been part of the first iteration of JHSSMUN last year, I am pleased to have the opportunity to ensure that this conference will live up to its title of the largest single-day conference in BC yet again. Whether it be through selecting intriguing topics, developing effective background guides, or troubleshooting the logistics of this event, the JHSSMUN team this year has worked hard to allow you all to experience the best of Model United Nations. It is our hope that this conference will inspire and nurture a passion for Model United Nations, and be an inclusive space for all our delegates.

As you prepare for the conference, I encourage you to thoroughly research your assigned delegation and committee topics and to also familiarize yourself with the rules of procedure. This will undoubtedly enhance the quality of the debate and your experience.

The JHSSMUN team and I are dedicated to ensuring the comfort of each delegate so please do not hesitate to contact me at jhssmun.sg@gmail.com if you have any questions or concerns whatsoever. I look forward to seeing you all!

Dilara Priyankarage

Secretary-General, Jhssmun 2024
