United Nations Security Council

Russo-Ukraine War

COMMITTEE OVERVIEW

Enter the United Nations Security Council (UNSC), the primary global body for maintaining international peace and security. The UNSC, composed of 15 member nations, addresses critical issues that threaten world stability.

In this session, the UNSC will focus on the Russo-Ukraine War, a pressing conflict with far-reaching implications. Delegates will grapple with the complexities of the situation, navigating diplomatic tensions, humanitarian concerns, and potential security threats. Tasked with proposing resolutions and seeking diplomatic solutions, delegates must collaborate to mitigate the impact of the conflict and restore stability in the region. This committee offers a challenging and dynamic Model UN experience for seasoned delegates, providing a platform to engage with real-world geopolitical issues.

Further inquiries regarding this committee, position papers, and general questions can be submitted to jhssmununsc@gmail.com.
